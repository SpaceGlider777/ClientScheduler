Client Scheduler

The purpose of this application is to manage customer data and schedule customer appointments.
The application conforms to business rules such as being only able to schedule appointments during
business hours and preventing overlapping appointments. Users can also view three different reports
that provide valuable information about the data.

Directions: Launch the application in the IDE. This will bring you to the login page where you can login
with username "test" and password "test". Alternatively you can login with username "admin" and password "admin".
After logging in, you will be brought to the dashboard and there will be three tabs you can choose from.
The first tab is "Customers" where you can add, update, and delete customers. The second tab is "Appointments"
where you can add, update, and delete appointments. The third tab is "Reports" where you can view three
different kinds of reports.

The third report shows the number of customers based on country and division. This will show users where
most of the customers are located and can be used for analytics.
